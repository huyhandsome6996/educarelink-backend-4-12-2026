# EduCareLink Privacy Policy

**Last updated**: 2026-07-23

EduCareLink ("we", "us", or "our") operates the EduCareLink mobile application (the "Service"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and what choices you have.

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide
- **Account information**: name, email, phone number, password (hashed), role (parent / carepartner / admin)
- **Profile information**: avatar, date of birth, address, identity document (CCCD) images, qualifications/certificates (for carepartners)
- **Task information**: task description, child's age/grade, schedule, location, budget
- **Communication**: chat messages with AI chatbot, help center inquiries

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically
- **Location data**: real-time GPS coordinates while a task is in progress (only after explicit consent from carepartner, and only visible to the parent who created the task)
- **Device information**: device model, OS version, battery level, network type (only while a task is in progress)
- **Usage data**: app interactions, crash reports, performance metrics

### 1.3 Information from Third Parties
- Google / Facebook (only if you choose OAuth sign-in — currently disabled)

## 2. How We Use Information

- To match parents with suitable carepartners via AI recommendations
- To enable real-time location tracking during active tasks (safety feature)
- To send SOS alerts and geofence notifications
- To verify carepartner identity and qualifications (moderation)
- To resolve disputes and complaints
- To improve our AI moderation and recommendation models
- To comply with legal obligations

## 3. Information Sharing

We **never sell** your data. We share information only:
- With the counterparty in a task (parent ↔ carepartner), limited to what is necessary for that task
- With our hosting provider (Render) and AI provider (Google Gemini) for processing
- With law enforcement when legally compelled
- In aggregate, anonymized form for analytics

## 4. Data Retention

- Active account data: retained while your account is active
- Completed task data: retained 24 months for dispute resolution, then anonymized
- Location data: deleted 30 days after task completion
- Identity documents: retained while account active, deleted within 90 days of account deletion

## 5. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your data
- Correct inaccurate data
- Delete your account and associated data
- Export your data in machine-readable format
- Withdraw consent for location tracking at any time (tracking stops immediately)

To exercise these rights, email **support@educarelink.app**.

## 6. Data Security

- All passwords are hashed using PBKDF2 with high iterations
- All API traffic is encrypted via HTTPS / TLS 1.2+
- Tokens are stored in device SecureStore (encrypted keystore on Android, Keychain on iOS)
- Identity documents are access-controlled — only verified admins can review them

## 7. Children's Privacy

EduCareLink is intended for users 18+. Parents use the app to find carepartners for their children, but children do not use the app directly. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 18.

## 8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in Vietnam, the United States (Render, Google Gemini), or other countries where our service providers operate. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We will notify you of material changes via in-app notification and/or email at least 30 days before they take effect.
